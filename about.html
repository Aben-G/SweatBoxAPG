About SweatBox APG

Welcome to SweatBox APG - Addis Ababa's premier performance gym and the first CrossFit-accredited box in Ethiopia. We combine internationally-certified training with a vibrant community atmosphere to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Who We Are

SweatBox APG is Ethiopia's first CrossFit-accredited performance gym, featuring internationally certified trainers and state-of-the-art equipment. Our mission is to deliver exceptional fitness experiences through expert coaching and community support.
